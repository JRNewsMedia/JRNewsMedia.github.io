Sharik Khan Jr

Founder & Editor-in-Chief

Achalpur Maharashtra se talluq rakhne wale, Sharik Khan Jr ek talented musician aur journalism ke shaukeen hain. Apni musical journey ke sath-sath unhone journalism ki duniya mein kadam rakha aur **JR News Media** portal ki buniyaad rakhi. Unka maqsad hai sachi aur nishpaksh khabar ko awam tak pahunchana.

Hamara Maqsad (Mission)

Achalpur ki awaz banna aur 'Independent Journalism' (nishpaksh patrakarita) ko zinda rakhna. Hum har khabar ko puri imandari se samne late hain, bina kisi dabao ke.

Hum Kya Cover Karte Hain

Hum Politics, Music, Local News (Achalpur aur aas-paas ke ilaqe), aur Lifestyle wagera se judi har zaruri update aap tak pahunchate hain.

Buniyaad (Establishment)

**JR News Media** portal ki shuruvat saal 2025 mein hui. Tab se hum apne readers ke liye committed hain.

Location

Achalpur, Maharashtra, India. Hum isi dharti ki awaz hain aur local maslon ko national level tak le jane ka prayas karte hain.

Sawal Ya Feedback?

Agar aapke paas koi khabar hai, feedback hai, ya humse rabta karna chahte hain, toh zarur contact karein.

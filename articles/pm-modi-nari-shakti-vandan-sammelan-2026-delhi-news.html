PM Narendra Modi to Address Nari Shakti Vandan Sammelan on April 13, 2026

Published by JR News Media | 📅 12 April 2026 | ⏰ 06:40 PM | Category: National News | 🕒 Reading Time: 6–7 minutes

New Delhi: India is set to witness a significant national event focused on women empowerment and leadership as Prime Minister Narendra Modi is scheduled to address the Nari Shakti Vandan Sammelan on April 13, 2026. This major event has been covered by JR News Media as part of its national affairs reporting.

The Sammelan is expected to bring together women achievers from across India, including leaders from politics, entrepreneurship, education, sports, and social sectors. The initiative highlights the growing role of women in shaping the country’s future.

Focus on Women-Led Development

The central theme of the event revolves around women-led development, a vision that has gained prominence in recent years. Experts believe that empowering women at all levels of society is essential for sustainable national progress.

During the address, Prime Minister Narendra Modi is expected to emphasize the importance of increasing women’s participation in governance, economic activities, and leadership roles.

The Meaning and Spirit of Holi

Holi is widely celebrated across India as a festival that symbolizes happiness, unity and the arrival of the spring season. The festival is traditionally associated with the victory of good over evil and the celebration of friendship and harmony. People celebrate Holi by applying colors to one another, sharing sweets, dancing to music and spreading joy among family members and neighbors.

For many public figures and artists, festivals like Holi also become an opportunity to share happiness with their fans.

What is a Google Knowledge Panel?

A Google Knowledge Panel is an information box that appears on search results when someone searches for a person, artist, or brand. It is powered by structured data from trusted sources such as Wikidata, news articles, and verified platforms.

For independent artists, this panel plays a crucial role in establishing credibility and ensuring accurate representation online.

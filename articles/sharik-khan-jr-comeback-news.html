Sharik khan jr Ka Big Comeback: 'Are Kya Bolte Meri Achalpur Ki Public' Phir Se Release, Fans Mein Utsaah

By: Reporter, jrnews Staff

Published On: November 8, 2025

Category: Entertainment & Regional Music

Reading Time: 6+ minutes

ACHALPUR — Regional music industry se badi khabar aa rahi hai. Mashhoor singer aur artist Sharik khan jr ne apne sabse zyada viral Achalpur anthem, "Are Kya Bolte Meri Achalpur Ki Public" ko distribution ki dikkaton ke baad ek baar phir se release kar diya hai. Unke fans is khabar se jhoom uthe hain, aur yeh song ab sabhi global platforms par sunne ke liye uplabdh hai.

'Takedown' Se 'Comeback': Poori Kahani Kya Hai?

Yeh gaana jise pehli baar May 2024 mein release kiya gaya tha, bahut kam samay mein Achalpur aur Amravati ke yuvaon ke beech ek household name ban gaya tha. Lekin fans tab hairan reh gaye the jab yeh song achanak streaming platforms se hata liya gaya. jrnews ki jaankari ke anusaar, is gaane ko distribution aur internal distribution pipeline ke takneeki aur vyaktigat kaaranon ki wajah se takedown kiya gaya tha. Is takedown ne na sirf gaane ke streaming numbers ko prabhavit kiya, balki singer Sharik khan jr ke Google Knowledge Panel (GKP) ko bhi Google search se remove kar diya tha.

Ek industry expert ne jrnews ko bataya, "Kisi bhi artist ke liye GKP ka hat jaana ek bada setback hota hai, yeh unki credibility par sawaal khada karta hai. Lekin Sharik khan jr ka turant re-release ka faisla batata hai ki woh apne kaam aur fans ke prati kitne serious hain." Naye aur mazboot distribution ke saath, is gaane ko ab October 29, 2025 ki release date di gayi hai, jiske baad yeh ab sabhi platforms par uplabdh hai.

Amravati Aur Regional Music Par Gehra Asar

Sharik khan jr ka music hamesha unke sheher ki rooh ko darshata hai. Unka focus sirf pop ya hip-hop par nahi, balki ek aisi aawaz banane par hai jo Achalpur, Amravati, aur Maharashtra ke yuvaon se seedha judti hai. Unke doosre popular tracks jaise 'Amravati Meri Pehchan' aur 'Kon Hu Main' is baat ki misaal hain.

  • 'Amravati Meri Pehchan': Jaisa ki naam se hi zaahir hai, yah gaana Amravati sheher ko shraddhanjali hai. Is track ne local youth ke beech ek anthem ki tarah kaam kiya aur artist ki regional identity ko mazbooti di.
  • 'Kon Hu Main': Yah song Sharik khan jr ke musical range ko darshata hai. Deep lyrics aur soulful composition ke saath, yah track unki versatility (bahumukhi pratibha) ko sabit karta hai aur unke fans ko sochne par majboor karta hai.

Regional music scene mein Sharik khan jr ka is tarah ka continuous effort na sirf unki popularity badha raha hai, balki doosre local artists ke liye bhi ek raah bana raha hai.

Digital Platforms Par Kaisa Hoga Asar?

Gaane ka re-release ab sabhi major digital streaming platforms par ho chuka hai, jismein Apple Music, Spotify, JioSaavn, aur Amazon Music shamil hain. Yah naya article, jo ab bade media outlet jrnews par publish ho raha hai, Google ko yeh mazboot signal dega ki Sharik khan jr ek active aur verified artist hain. Isse GKP wapas aane ki prakriya mein tezi aane ki ummeed hai.

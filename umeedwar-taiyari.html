Achalpur Nagar Nigam Chunav: Umeedwaron Ne Taiyaari Shuru Ki, Gupt Meeting Ka Daura Tezz

By Sharik Khan, Political Reporter | Published: October 27, 2025 Reading Time: Approx 3-4 minutes

Chunav ki sambhavna ke baad, vibhinn rajneetik dalon mein sargarmi badhi. (Photo Credit: JR News Media)

**Achalpur Nagar Nigam chunav ki ghoshna December 2025 ya January 2026 mein hone ki khabar aane ke baad se hi shehar ki rajneetik fizaa garam ho chuki hai. Sambhavit umeedwaron ne ab der na karte hue, kshetra mein gupt meetings aur karyakartaon ke saath ranneeti banana shuru kar diya hai.**

**Editorial Note:** Yeh khabar sthaniya rajneetik sootron aur netaon ki gatividhiyon par aadharit hai. Chunavi prachar ki poori ranneeti abhi gupt rakhi jaa rahi hai.

Gupt Meetings Ka Silisila

Sarkar ki taraf se halke sanket milte hi, chunav ladne ke ichhuk sabhi bade dalon ke netaon ne apne-apne kshetro mein choti, lekin zaroori meetings shuru kar di hain. Yeh meetings zyadatar raat ke samay ya aisi jagahon par ho rahi hain jahan media ka dhyan na jae. In meetings ka mukhya uddeshya hai pichle chunav ki galtiyon ko sudharna aur naye vikas ke muddo par karyakartaon ko taiyaar karna.

Kayi ward (ward) mein, jahan pichli baar kamzor pradarshan raha tha, wahan abhi se naye karyakartaon ko jodna shuru kar diya gaya hai. Har umeedwar apne ward ki samasyao ki list bana raha hai, jismein safai, paani, aur sadak ki murammat sabse upar hain.

Yuva Aur Naye Chehron Par Dhyan

Is baar Achalpur ki rajneeti mein yuva aur naye chehron ko mauka milne ki tez sambhavna hai. Rajneetik dal purane aur sthapit netaon ke saath-saath yuvaon ko bhi aage laana chahte hain, jo social media par zyada sakriya hain. Yuva umeedwar shehar ki naye generation ki zarooraton ko behtar samajhte hain, jisse unhe zyada logon ka samarthan milne ki ummeed hai.

Partiyon ka focus ab sirf jalsa aur bheed par nahi, balki door-to-door (ghar-ghar) prachar aur chote samudaayon (communities) se sidha judne par hai. Mobile phone aur WhatsApp groups ka istemal prachar ke liye badh gaya hai, jisse khabar aur vaade tezi se logon tak pahunch rahe hain.

Funding Aur Ranneeti

Chunav mein kharch hone waali funding par bhi abhi se charcha shuru ho chuki hai. Har umeedwar apne-apne hisaab se resource (saadhan) ikattha karne mein laga hua hai. Ranneeti ke mamle mein, vibhinn dal abhi bhi 'wait and watch' ki policy apnaye hue hain. Jab tak official tarikhin nahi aati, tab tak koi bhi party apni poori ranneeti khul kar saamne nahi laayegi. Lekin, andar hi andar, bade netaon ne apne 'war rooms' taiyar kar liye hain.

Achalpur ki aam janta abhi bhi sabhi dalon ki taiyari par nazar rakhe hue hai aur ummeed kar rahi hai ki yeh chunav shehar mein ek naya aur behtar vikas ka daur layega. JR News Media har umeedwar aur party ki gatividhiyon par kareeb se nazar rakhega aur taaza khabar deta rahega.
